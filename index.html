UC Riverside · Riccomagno Neurodevelopmental Lab

AAV-Mediated Gene Delivery to the Brain

tested different promoters with adeno-associated viruses (AAV) to deliver genetic cargo to the brain, discovering that a bioinformatic approach to identifying promoters can drive reporter expression in specific neural cell types.

also conducted a project testing a transgenic mouse line to study axonal pruning, yielding insights into the molecular mechanisms underlying neurodevelopment.

tldr: figuring out how to precisely target the right cells in the brain using engineered viruses.

projects

building things outside the lab

Python · Market Analysis

Cancer Market Sizing Tool

built a Python-based market sizing tool that models the addressable patient population for cancer diagnostics across geographies and cancer types. the tool lets assumptions shift dynamically — swap in a different region or indication, and the numbers update accordingly.

incorporated national statistics across multiple cancer indications to size both mail-in and lab-based testing markets. the analysis revealed a combined addressable market of ~109 million people in the US alone, with meaningful variation by risk category and testing modality.

tldr: turned a spreadsheet problem into a flexible, reproducible tool for market sizing in oncology diagnostics.

Python pandas oncology market sizing

output: market size breakdown by perceived risk & threshold — 109.31M total addressable population
